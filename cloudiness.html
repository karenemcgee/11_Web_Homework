Cloudiness and Latitude

This plot depicts how cloudy a location is based on its latitude. As you can see, cloudiness does not seem to change based on latitude. Cities between -60 degrees and 80 degrees range from no cloudiness on that particular day to 100% cloudiness but there is no one latitude range that has higher or lower cloudiness.
